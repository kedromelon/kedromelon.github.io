Kedromelon

Solo project, focused on energetic, minimalist chipmusic composed and performed using Little Sound DJ for the Nintendo Game Boy. I have released two EPs and have been included on the Chiptunes = WIN: Volume 1 compilation. I also won first place in the 2012 noisechannel.org LSDJ Showdown. My performances have been featured at Pulsewave, I/O, and Byte Nyte.
