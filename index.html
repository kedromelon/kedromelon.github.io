DISCOBALL

Final project, made in about five weeks in a group of four people. I was primarily responsible for programming and sound, but also had input in game design. The game is designed for two teams of two using Xbox 360 controllers, but it can also be attempted with the keyboard. On the keyboard, players one to four are controlled by WASD, TFGH, IJKL, and [;'ENTER respectively. The keys around each directional control (ex: QEZX for player one) replace the Xbox 360 controllers A, B, X, and Y buttons. Use spacebar to start the game.
